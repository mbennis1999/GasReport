Report a Gas Emergency

If you suspect a gas leak or have a gas emergency, leave the area immediately and call us from a safe location.

Emergency Number:

1-800-123-4567

Available 24/7

What to Do in a Gas Emergency

  • Leave the area immediately.
  • Call 1-800-123-4567 from a safe location.
  • Do not operate any electrical devices, including phones.
  • Do not light a match, candle, or any flame.
